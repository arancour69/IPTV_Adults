## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Streams aren't loading
**A:** Try these steps:
1. Verify your internet connection
2. Restart your IPTV player
3. Check that the URL is correct
4. Try a different playlist file
5. Clear your player's cache
6. Ensure your player is up-to-date

### Q: Getting buffering issues
**A:** Possible solutions:
1. Check your internet speed (minimum 5 Mbps)
2. Try a lower quality stream if available
3. Close other bandwidth-heavy apps
4. Try a wired connection instead of WiFi
5. Contact your ISP if speeds are consistently low

### Q: Can I use these streams commercially?
**A:** No. These are for personal/private use only. Commercial use requires proper licensing.

### Q: Are these streams legal?
**A:** Verify compliance with local laws and content provider terms of service in your jurisdiction.

### Q: How often are streams updated?
**A:** We maintain and update playlists regularly. Check the [CHANGELOG](CHANGELOG.md) for recent updates.

### Q: Which player should I use?
**A:** All listed players (Kodi, VLC, Tivimate, IPTV) work well. Choose based on:
- **Kodi** - Best for home theater setups
- **VLC** - Most compatible, works anywhere
- **Tivimate** - Optimized for Android
- **IPTV Player** - Dedicated IPTV experience

### Q: Can I add my own streams?
**A:** Yes! See [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md) for guidelines.

## 🐛 Reporting Issues

### For Broken Streams
1. Check if it's already reported: [View issues](../../issues)
2. Include in your report:
   - Which playlist file
   - Channel/stream name
   - Your player and OS
   - When you discovered the issue

[**Report a Broken Stream**](../../issues/new?template=bug_report.md)

### For Stream Suggestions
1. Search existing [feature requests](../../issues?q=label:enhancement)
2. Test the stream to ensure it works
3. Provide the stream URL and details

[**Request a Stream**](../../issues/new?template=feature_request.md)

### For Other Issues
1. Search [existing issues](../../issues)
2. Provide clear description and steps to reproduce

[**Open an Issue**](../../issues/new)

## 📝 Creating a Good Issue

### For Bugs:
```
Title: [BUG] Brief description
- Player: [name and version]
- OS: [system and version]
- Steps to reproduce: [numbered list]
- Expected result: [what should happen]
- Actual result: [what actually happens]
```

### For Features:
```
Title: [REQUEST] Brief description
- What: [detailed description]
- Why: [why this is needed]
- Details: [URLs, stream info, etc.]
```

## ⚖️ Code of Conduct

When seeking or providing support:
- ✅ Be respectful and professional
- ✅ Search before asking duplicate questions
- ✅ Provide detailed information
- ✅ Follow up on solutions that work
- ❌ Don't share personal credentials
- ❌ Avoid offensive language
- ❌ Don't spam issues/discussions
